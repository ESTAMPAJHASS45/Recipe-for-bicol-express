What is Bicol Express?

Originating from the Bicol region of the Philippines, Bicol Express is a rich and spicy stew of pork simmered in coconut milk with chilies and shrimp paste. Customize the heat by adjusting the amount of siling labuyo (bird‚Äôs eye chilies).

Serve & Store

Serving suggestions

  • Best with hot steamed jasmine rice.
  • Add blanched sitaw (string beans) or pechay for veggies.
  • For a crispy twist, serve with lechon kawali on top.

Storage

Refrigerate in an airtight container up to 3 days. Gently reheat on the stove; add a splash of coconut milk if too thick.
